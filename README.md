# ?? AI-Powered FMCG Retail Intelligence Platform

> Enterprise-grade retail analytics system built on real-world Amul retailer survey data  
> **500 retailers · 40 columns · 6 zones · Jaipur Market**

---

## ?? Business Problem

Amul's Cheese & Tofu (C&T) product line suffers from critically low retailer penetration
(15.2%) despite near-universal refrigeration availability (98.8%) in the Jaipur market.

This platform identifies awareness gaps, competitor threats, and high-opportunity retailers
using AI, NLP, and ML to drive a data-backed distribution strategy.

---

## ?? Key Findings

- **57% of retailers** have never heard of Amul C&T — an awareness crisis, not a demand crisis
- **FruBon beats Amul** in rep visit quality ratings (40% vs Amul) and trade promotions (48%)
- **Only 18% of retailers** have ever received a trade promotion on Amul Dahi
- **69.4% complaint rate** — sourness (32%) and packaging (15%) are top product issues
- **C-Scheme zone** represents the highest-margin expansion opportunity
